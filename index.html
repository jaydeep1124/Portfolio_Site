Substation Design Engineer at RCM Technologies with a Master of Engineering from University of Windsor. My core work is physical substation design — AIS/GIS yard layouts, equipment arrangement, XLPE cable routing and duct bank design, grounding grid systems, and site-survey-to-drawing workflows using point-cloud data.

I build practical, code-compliant designs that translate stakeholder requirements into constructable, on-site deliverables across MV, HV, EHV, and UHV voltage levels. Previously engineered MV power assemblies at Rockwell Automation and led distribution & grounding design for a 455MW solar project at UGVCL.
